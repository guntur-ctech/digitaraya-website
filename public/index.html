Halosis

halosis.co.id

Halosis was part of Digitaraya’s AI batch. Thanks to Digitaraya, Halosis was connected to relevant industry partners and given access to technical resources and expert mentors. Halosis provides virtual assistance services (chatbot) to 3,000 SMEs or online sellers in Indonesia, which can process business orders and other management tasks in Bahasa Indonesia and English. Aside from its chatbot, the company also uses text analytics, NLP, machine learning, and image recognition to cater to customer needs.
