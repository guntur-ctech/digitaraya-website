Responding to COVID-19 together

Digitaraya is committed to help startups in Indonesia and the wider community impacted by the COVID-19 pandemic during this challenging time. We have gathered essential information, best practices, productivity tools, network initiatives, and government updates and regulations related to COVID-19. Our objective is to provide relevant, verified information and to ease the process of finding the right information needed by founders and the tech community.

Best Practices

In this unsettling and unprecedented situation, we are all being forced to recalibrate our efforts to focus on sustaining our businesses. To help founders and startups adjust to the new reality, Digitaraya has pulled together best practices from our network, thought leaders, venture capitalists and startups, in the form of wisdom, templates, and guidance.
